Alan Dickman
Solution Architect
IBM Watson & Cloud Platform Group

BIO

Alan Dickman is a Solution Architect, Executive Consultant and Technical Sales Specialist on theWatson & Cloud Platform team of IBM North America. He supports the specification and sale of business solutions based on IBM blockchain to state & local governments, healthcare and life sciences organizations across the United States.

In 2010, Mr. Dickman received a Global Technical Award for his contributions to IBM's Advanced Metering Infrastructure business solution offering.
